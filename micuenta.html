Garbarino.com

UI, UX, Ilustration

The project
Garbarino is one of the main retail companies in Argentina. And the past year it started to invest a lot in their ecommerce platform, making a lot of focus in UX/UI design. One of the projects I was assigned to do in this new era was the user account and login session.

The challenge
Since this it was going to be the first version of the section, most of the services we wanted to include were not available at first. So we try to focus on an MVP with at least all the “musts” we should have, working side by side with the development team and trying to achieve that objective. The MVP included favorites section, history, my profile and shipping tracking.

Role:
UX, UI designer

UX SHOT

Branding, Webdesign

The project
Uxshot is a side project I started with a friend which purpose was to expand the spanish ux community and explain to those who were not working in this field and wanted to know what it was about, what ux is and how the experts here in Latin America are applying it in their companies.

The challenge
To accomplished this project besides having to contact all the people we wanted to interview, we needed to have an identity. So we started by creating our brand, landing page, facebook page and all kinds of communication tools and channels to make this happen. We wanted the visual identity to be fresh and easy to remember. Here in Argentina the education in UX is not so developed, so there are not enough places to learn about this discipline, that’s why we wanted our identity to be adopted especially by young students, curious about what this UX thing is…and what better way that learning straight from the people that are doing it, right?

How can I help you accomplish your project?

UI/UX Designer | Brand Desginer | Motion Graphics Enthusiast

A great user expereince (UX) design
I've been building digital experiences for a few years now, and working side by side with UX and Developement teams from different companies has given me the experience to understand the best ways to approach the design and experience challenges of the projects I've been involved. I believe putting the user at the centre of the design is the key to make every project perform at the best of it's capacity and make them successful.

Building better brands
Along side a well developed User Experience there always has to be a well crafted brand. Understanding how to communicate genuinely with your target audience is something I am passionate about and that I like to explore in every product I work.
A brand is not only a logo, it's the way the customer comes in contact with your company, having this in mind is the best way to make your users adopt your product above all the rest.

Personal info
I'm an Argentinian designer who specialises in digital experiences and product design. I studied Graphic Design in the University of Buenos Aires, and have been expanding my knoledge in everything new that comes up in this craft that I love so much since then :)
